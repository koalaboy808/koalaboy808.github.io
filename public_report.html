Public Report:

Data Visualization for Hawaii's Education

[Data Visualization] [R] [RStudio]







Project lead in Data Analysis and Visualization (R)

Our data team at the Hawaii Department of Education was tasked to tackle the large-scale project of creating customized reports for each of the 286 schools under the new policy framework, “Strive HI”. In previous years, the DOE had created unintelligible matrices of data. The new framework, however, required unequivocal transparency.

I personally lead the development of producing comprehensive yet concise visualizations for each school. I developed the code in R which would be used for all the future annual reports. Moreover, the iterative approach refined the structure of the assessment data pipeline. I designed for simplicity without losing any critical information, as the report demanded consideration for a gamut of interested stakeholders—students, principals, community members, and non-profit organizations.
